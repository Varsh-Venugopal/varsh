quinoa salad

a quinoa and chickpea salad. Start by mixing cooked quinoa with boiled chickpeas, diced cucumber, red bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes. Add freshly chopped parsley for a burst of flavor. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and black pepper. Toss everything together and serve it chilled or at room temperature for a refreshing and nutritious dish.
